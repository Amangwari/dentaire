Clinic History

The Dental Center takes pride in being amongst the finest dental care centers since 1978. Founded by the renowned dentist Dr. I.S. Mehta, with a vast experience of 46 years, our clinic has grown into a center of excellence for dental and facial treatments.

From a small, private practice at Holy Angel Hospital, Vasant Vihar, it has been a journey of growth to expansion into two of New Delhi and Gurugram's most prestigious hospitals, Manipal Hospitals. For years, we have participated in providing top-notch dental treatment, embracing the latest technologies to ensure our patients receive only the highest-level treatment. We value the trust of every patient with a patient-centered approach and continue to serve with long-built relationships.

Our Team

Dr. I.S. Mehta – Founder & Senior Dental Specialist

With experience of 46 years in dentistry, Dr. Mehta is indeed a stalwart in the field of dentistry. Mission & Vision

Our Mission

It is the mission of the Dental Center to provide quality, patient-centered dentistry with compassion and excellence, using evidence-based learning and advanced technology. We provide a caring and comfortable atmosphere that leads to positive dental experiences and healthy lifestyles for all our patients. We strive to reinforce the natural beauty in every smile through the restoration and maintenance of optimal oral health by means of knowledge, state-of-the-art technology, and experience.

Our Vision

Our ambitious vision is to make recognized leadership in dental care, through innovation and a superior patient experience. We seek to continually raise the benchmark of dental practices by integrating state-of-the-art technologies, further developing our skills, and expanding our service profile. It is our hope that each and every patient we see at our clinic goes away with a smile on his face and an improved lifestyle, feeling well taken care of.

Core Values

Excellence

In our practice, we strive for excellence in every respect, from clinical care to the interaction with the patient. As a team, we strongly work at self-improvement using new techniques and technologies in our treatments to assure best possible results for our patients.

Compassion

We treat every patient with kindness, understanding, and respect. We understand how some patients feel any apprehension and also try to provide a relaxed and supportive atmosphere that would make dental visits free of stress and comfortable.

Integrity

Honesty and transparency lay the foundation for our practice. We earn patients' trust through clarity in communication, treatment plans devised based on ethical grounds, and personalized care suited to needs that are unique to each individual.

Innovation

Embracing the future of dentistry is key to our philosophy. We are committed to using the latest technology and techniques available in order for our patients to receive the most advanced and effective treatments.

Teamwork

Collaboration is key to our success. Our multidisciplinary team works as one cohesive unit to ensure that every patient benefits from the collective expertise of highly qualified professionals. Care is provided through the coordination and integration of skills and knowledge.

Patient-Centered Care

Our patients remain at the center of everything we do. We make sure their needs, comfort, and well-being are always on the front burner through our attention to their goals. Every treatment plan is designed individually to meet the specific needs of the patient, whether physical or emotional, toward a positive experience and successful outcome.
